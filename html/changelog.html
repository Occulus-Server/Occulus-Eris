26 May 2023

WeNeedMorePhoron updated:

  • Armor now display flat damage reduction. This was added in 23 months ago as part of the ErisMed 3 port but was not visible or knowable to player, until now.
  • Artist Bench no longer require Plasteel.
  • Alt-click to open a container. Description Information (Seen in examine tab) added to instruct you.

25 May 2023

Michiyamenotehifunana updated:

  • Added the Bio-engineer role! Makes custom aberrant organs and organ mods.
  • Added aberrant organs! Special organs that do certain things in response to stimuli.
  • Added organ mods! Simple stuff to tack onto your existing organs to make them better.

RadiantFlash7 updated:

  • subtle emote

WeNeedMorePhoron updated:

  • Alternative 2x Kitsune tail, 3, 7, 9 kitsune tails. 9 kitsune tail is technically 9-in-one but not today. Waggable soon?
  • Stomach Pump added to sleeper. Works similarly to dialysis but clear out your stomach's content. Will nuke your nutrition on use (10 / 400 nutrition removed per tick).
  • Craftable Pipebomb made with plasma cell
  • HESH Rockets. RPG-7 package starts with 4 out of 12 rockets being HESH. HESH rocket have worse blast but send a lot of fragments to the other side of a room.
  • White phosphorus grenades, accessible to traitor for now.
  • Normal HE Rocket nerfed a bit, upstream parity. Still kill with a direct hit.
  • Stinger Grenades sprite changed to match upstream, fragmentation grenades now cause a 2 tiles small blast around itself.
  • Grenades dropping on someone from above should now bonk off someone's head.
  • Grenade fragments now ricochet making them much deadlier, especially in small room.
  • Grenade now generally produce 1/3 the fragment but deals 3x the damage. Upstream parity, slight buff, frag grenades and pipebomb has a blast radius of 2x. Grenade deadliness against unarmored people is practically unchanged, grenade deadliness against CAS Voidsuit / Deepflare goes from 0 damage to 110 at 0 - 1 tile range, rapidly dropping off to 11 - 30 at 2 - 3 tiles range.
  • Fire stacks are much deadlier, approximately by 2x, making being on fire less ignorable. They also put themselves out slowly.
  • Falling item that deals less than 5 damage won't knock you down anymore (Tiny and Small Item)
  • A crash that can happen with grenade launcher launched grenade.

Zenitheevee updated:

  • Replaces the circuit disk overwrites with a better addition
  • Added S5 Shotgun mags and designs to respective disks
  • Added Stoat Shotgun and disk, Shotgun available in the Frozenstar Vendor for 2100 credits
  • Added Magazine kit compatible with Regulators and Gladstones
  • Allows pump-action shotguns to support mags.

WeNeedMorePhoron updated:

  • Nurse (Kango) spiders no longer remotely inject people when ATTEMPTING to attack you, only when they bite.

20 May 2023

WeNeedMorePhoron updated:

  • When Warrior is voted in mid round, it gives 120 roleset point and will almost immediately spawn an antag.
  • Dice now have examine text for how to use them and can be operated by alt-click.
  • Toxin reference in code no longer mistakenly adds tar, a toxin 40x as deadly as baseline toxin. This mean that maint food, microwave burnt mess, some random plants, rejected organs, incompatible blood, maint loot etc. no longer give tar but give baseline toxin.
  • Phoron splash on people cause chemical burns again, phoron spilled on ground cause phoron gas to appear potentially causing fire and atmos alarm, phoron splashed on people increase the fire stack.

18 May 2023

Michiyamenotehifunana updated:

  • Diving. Hold C in hotkey mode while moving to dive in any direction. You can dive on tables, railings, and low walls. Diving into glass hurts you, but it also hurts the glass. (You'll get hurt more than the glass does)
  • RIG ricochets. Your RIGs can now deflect bullets.
  • Throwing knives. Craft them.
  • Cover rework, low walls, tables, and security barricades now block bullets somewhat more immersively.
  • Bullets now travel up and down stairs.
  • In order to accommodate diving, you can now hold items while laying down. You are unable to attack or use items while laying down, however. There is also now a delay for getting up from laying down.

WeNeedMorePhoron updated:

  • Adds in Myosynaptizine - a chemical made from Polystem and Synaptizine that heals nerves and muscles. Mild overdose risk.
  • Time of death now display properly on health analyzer. Report if time is very off.
  • Health Analyzer now displays husked corpse as "Severe tissue damage detected. Resuscitation not possible."
  • Health analyzer now displays in big red text when you can defib someone.
